What I do

Promotional Email

The purpose of the promotional email is to let customers and subscribers know about special discounts on certain products and even by one get one type of offers.

Newsletter Email

The purpose of the newsletter email is to keep the subscriber updated on the latest news and new products coming out for the company. It's also a good way to let the subscriber in on any upcoming promotions or sales without giving too much information away.

Transactional Email

This type of email shows the customer the details of their purchase such as, the method of payment, the items purchased, the time and date of purchase, and the last four digits of the credit/debit card used.
